Embracing the Echoes of the Future

In the hush of night, the whispers flow,

A gentle promise where dreams will grow.

Through shadows deep and stars that gleam,

Lies the quiet echo of a future unseen.

Each step we take, a path unfolds,

A tale unwritten, yet to be told.

The winds may howl, the skies may weep,

Yet hope persists, in hearts it keeps.

For tomorrow whispers, soft and true,

A canvas awaiting the strokes of you.

Paint it bold, with hues of light,

For even the darkest gives way to bright.

So heed the call, embrace the flow,

Tomorrow awaits, let its whispers show.
